ABOUT US —

WHO WE ARE

"Unity is our strength, diversity is our power" DiversiBe raises awareness of equality in the workplace in Belgium. We are a team of people that believes in the power of diversity. We are collective working force to create awareness of underrepresented communities into the business.

TEAM —

WHAT WE DO

We promote the development and advancement of underrepresented groups for eg: Women, Foreigners, Immigrants, Undergraduates, LGBTQIAP+ by offering professional help to improve their skills for the job market. We do that through entrepreneurship, working on unaddressed societal issues while building their skills.
